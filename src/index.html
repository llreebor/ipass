Unlocking the Future of Access Control

Pass is a technology leader in secure access control. Discover our state-of-the-art solutions and elevate your security to the next level.

  • iPass "universal readers" work with all access control systems, supporting smartcards and smartphones plus Wiegand and OSDP.

  • iPass "intuitive mobility" uses cryptographic credentials that allow iOS and Android smartphones to work alongside regular smartcards.

Universal Readers

iPass Multi-Technology Readers

Experience the future of access control with iPass Multi-Technology Access Readers. Our advanced access readers support all access control systems and cards, offering flexibility plus high security. With support for Bluetooth smartphone and NFC smart card authentication, and connectivity via Wiegand and OSDP Secure Channel, our readers ensure that only authorized personnel can access your facilities. Stylish ergonomic design, in multiple form factors and capabilities, makes them a perfect fit for all access requirement.

Unbreakable Security

iPass Cryptographic Access Cards

Elevate your security level with iPass Cryptographic Access Cards. Utilizing high-strength public key cryptography, our access cards provide unbreakable credentials that significantly enhance the security of any access solution. A public key that can be exposed without risk and a private key that never leaves the card eliminate the risk of duplication or forgery, plus our uniquely simple “no PKI” technology, make them the perfect choice to enhance any access control system.

Intuitive Mobility

iPass Mobile Credentials

Leverage Bluetooth smartphones or watches as secure access credentials with iPass Mobile Credentials. Our intuitive iPass App, available for both iOS and Android, allows for quick and easy enrollment and credential generation. With support for multi-factor authentication, including PIN codes, facial recognition, and fingerprint matching, our mobile credentials offers flexible security levels to meet the most stringent security requirements
