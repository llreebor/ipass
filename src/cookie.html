1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (such as login details, language, and display preferences) over time, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time you visit.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following types of cookies on our website:

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of our website. Without these cookies, certain features of the website may not function properly.

2.2 Performance Cookies: Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. These cookies allow us to improve the performance and functionality of our site.

2.3 Functionality Cookies: Functionality cookies enable our website to provide enhanced features and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.

2.4 Targeting Cookies: These cookies track your activity on our website to help us deliver more relevant content. They may also be used to track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.
